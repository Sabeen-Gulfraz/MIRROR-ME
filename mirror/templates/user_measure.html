To be followed !

  1. Wear skin-tight clothing before standing in front of the camera.
  2. Turn on the camera and stand facing it at a distance that allows your full body to be visible. If your full body is not visible, the measurements cannot be taken.
  3. Background and clothes color should be different.
  4. Stand in a well-lit area for better results.
  5. The camera will capture your live image to extract measurements. Your image will be deleted immediately after processing.
  6. Once the measurements are extracted, then they are automatically saved to your profile and can be used when you are placing an order.
  7. If you want to place an order then you have to extract your body measurements first. your latest measurements will be used in order placement.
