About Us

Mirror Me is the home of clients and Tailors. We are here to bridge the gap between Tailors and their clients to create the perfect outfit for any special occasion. This platform will enable clients to find, & review Tailors within their vicinity.

This platform is for both tailors and their clients with different interfaces.

Clients can search and select nearby tailors for them. And there is no need to visit tailor physically because "Mirror Me" allow you to extract your live body measurements by using your device camera, also you can save them for further use.

Tailors can see your measurements in your order details and design custom fit clothes for you. Your clothes Pick-up and delivery is also managed by us.
